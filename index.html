Optional Google Drive Sync
Back up and sync your portfolio across devices — completely optional.

SatLedger offers optional Google Drive integration so you can back up your portfolio data and access it from any browser. This feature is entirely optional — the app works fully without it.

What data
Your transactions, wallets, settings, and encrypted vault notes. This is the same data stored in your browser's localStorage.
Where
Data is stored in Google Drive's appDataFolder — a private, app-specific storage space that is not visible in your regular Drive files.
Scope
Only the drive.appdata scope is requested. SatLedger cannot see or access any of your other Google Drive files.
Control
You can disconnect at any time from Settings, or revoke access in your Google Account permissions.
